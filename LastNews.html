Soccer

The top 12 teams of europe are trying to downgrade the UEFA Champions League Cup, while trying to create a new cup named the Super League, consisting 15 founder teams, plus 5 more that will be incorporated according to their results and efforts at the end of the season. This new cup is creatin a lot of controversy since it would mean that smaller teams would not be able to progress more because of salary differences and all spectators and TV streamings going into the Super League. The FIFA and UEFA are trying everything they can do to stop the creation of this cup

Football (NFL)

The NFL has just recently approved to change the rules for the 2021 season. They highlight a relaxation to the rules of numbering jerseys by positions in the field and expansion of the powers of the review officer for the coming regular season. Los oficiales de repetición no podrán lanzar pañuelos por su cuenta ni revertir llamadas por su cuenta. Pero, ahora podrán ofrecer consejo a los réferis con base a lo que han visto en las repeticiones durante la transmisión con respecto a posesión, pases completos o interceptados, la colocación del balón respecto a la línea lateral o línesa final, y si una jugador ha hecho down por contacto.
